About me

Hi, I'm Ash - mum, special education teacher and creator.

I love spending quality time with my family, cooking, pilates/gym, being in nature, coffee, music and just having a bit of fun. Most of all I love sharing my life through vlogs, hauls, chit chats and all the rest over on TikTok!

My content style blends everyday storytelling and authentic moments that feel realistic, relatable and genuine. I love creating content that feels warm, inviting and easy for audiences to connect with.

From the chaos of mum-life to teacher-life to everyday moments, I bring a unique perspective that aligns with your everyday viewer. My content allows brands to show up in ways that feel realistic, useful and enjoyable to watch allowing viewers to organically engage with products.
